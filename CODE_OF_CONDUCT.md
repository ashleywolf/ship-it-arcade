## Enforcement

Community moderators are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may:

- **Edit or remove** comments, issues, commits, code, wiki edits, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- **Warn** community members engaging in inappropriate behavior
- **Temporarily or permanently ban** anyone whose behavior is deemed harmful, threatening, or repeatedly in violation of these standards

All moderation decisions are made in the interest of keeping this community safe, welcoming, and fun for everyone.
